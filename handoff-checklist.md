# Handoff Checklist

Before anything goes to a client, verify:

- Client name and company are correct.
- Problem statement matches what the client actually said.
- Offer does not invent pricing, guarantees, or timelines.
- Scope is clear enough that both sides know what is included.
- Missing information is marked clearly.
- Email is labeled as a draft until Gili approves it.
- No private client data is moved into external tools without approval.

## Approval Prompt

Approval needed: should this be sent as-is, edited first, or held?
